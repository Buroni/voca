Privacy Policy

Cookies

Vocabin.net uses cookies for security reasons to confirm that users of our services are indeed on vocabin.net. This prevents unauthorised use of our API and related services from third-party platforms.

Vocabin.net also uses Google Analytics to track anonymous user traffic and behaviour. Google Analytics itself uses cookies to implement these services.

User Accounts

All visitors to Vocabin.net are associated with an anonymous user account for the duration of the browser session. This account holds no personal information and is used solely to authorise the visitor to use the Vocabin API.
